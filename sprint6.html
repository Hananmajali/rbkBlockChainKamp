Sprint 6: GreenChain


  • In this sprint you will build your full DAPP And Implement Your Idea
  • You can build your own standard or you can use ERC721 or ERC20
  • You are free to choose your front end language to get better idea open truffle Boxes


    • The Project

    • start a new project using truffle you can Check the official documentation to get started truffle documentation
    • think about a good idea that solves a real problem and make use of ethereum to make the smart contract , you can use remix IDE to write the smart contracts.
    • use ganache to deploy your contracts
    • build a simple front end to make use of your contract you are free to use what ever technologies you like.
    • don't forget to push your code to github.
    • deploy your contracts to Reposten or any other test network
    • deploy your DAPP to any host service to have your DAPP online.
