DIY Blockchain

What better way to learn about blockchains than to build your own? In this sprint you will construct your own simple blockchain in the vein of Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies. Using cryptographic private/public key pairs, users will be able to create transactions sending funds from their public key to that of another user. Like any other blockchain, these transactions will become a permanent immutable part of the ledger, protected by the hashes that link each block to the one that came before it in the chain.
